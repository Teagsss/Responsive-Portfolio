About Me

I love playing drums, listening to music, going to concerts, working out, going out and at times, not doing anything but laying around. I've played the drums on and off for 15 years. I'm currenty not in a band but love playing covers to songs

A few random facts about myself: I had a beagle named Magic growing up, I have to take a shower before the gym, I love ketchup on my eggs, I broke my ankle wrestling my freind in college.
